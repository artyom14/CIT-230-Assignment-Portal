Contact Us

We have booking agents available to speak to twenty-four hours a day, six days a week. If you have any questions about pricing, long-term accommodations, amenities, or anything else, our agents are more than happy to assist you. We always appreciate feedback about the quality of our hotels. If you encounter any problems during your stay please don't hesitate to reach out to us.

Temple Inn & Suites was founded in 2010 in Rexburg, IF. We started with a single hotel serving patrons of the Washington, D.C. Temple but have since expanded to four different locations all across the United States. We are dedicated to providing quality lodgings for temple patrons, and in maintaining an atmosphere of peace and reverence for all who stay at our hotels. Our corporate headquarters are still located in Bethesda, on the ground floor of our hotel, and less than 10 minutes from the Washington, D.C. Temple.
